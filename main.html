Stock Options

Options are a different way to invest in stocks. Instead of buying and selling the stocks, you trade the right to buy and sell stocks at a certain price. You buy a call option if you believe that the price of the stock will go up. Having a call option will give you the right to buy the stock at a certain price. If you think that the stock price will go down, you can buy a put option, which will give you the right to sell the stock at a certain price. Unlike stocks, options must be bought in 100 increments. Buying one call option gives you the right to buy 100 stocks at the strike price. Also, options have an expiration date, which is the time the options must be used by or they become worthless. Options are more risky, which means that you can either earn or lose money very quickly.
